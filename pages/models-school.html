Model School Program
Our program for models consists of 31 classes, among them:
  • One lesson in the theory of photo-class and 5 photos
  • 2 lessons of style (theory and practice, where you will find a stylish look from your own wardrobe, to each photo shoot)
  • 8 lessons from the catwalk and posing
  • 6 lessons of acting skills
  • One lesson on restaurant etiquette + lunch in a restaurant
  • 2 lessons in nutrition
  • One lesson of visage
  • One lesson of cosmetology
  • One lesson on modeling theory
  • One meeting with the model
  • One lesson on diction
  • One English lesson (dictionary of terms)
